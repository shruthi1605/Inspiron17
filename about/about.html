U.V.C.E

Established in the year 1917 by Bharat Ratna Sir M Visvesvaraya, UVCE has acquired leading position in technical education. Titan among educational institutions, one of the oldest and the most venerated engineering colleges in Karnataka. It has unique mix of dedication towards academic and active industrial collaboration.

THE PLACEMENT OFFICE

The Training and Placement Office in UVCE, carries on its back a reputation. It is known to safeguard the professional careers of aspiring students, by getting companies of various cadres for recruitment. Besides, the TPO also undertakes a number of initiatives to train the students for a successful and vibrant corporate life. This includes seminars and workshops aimed at developing soft skills in students , as well as proficiency in general aptitude. Apart from this, we conduct the annual two day techno-management extravaganza - INSPIRON.

INSPIRON

Since its inception in 2009, Inspiron has been hosted by The Training and Placement Office, UVCE. With INSPIRON, UVCE provides its students an opportunity to hone as well as showcase their practical know - how by organizing events that focus on all the key areas for personal develop-ment of a student. This initiative from The Training and Placement Office gives every student platform to compete with the best minds not only within this college , but also with those from different colleges across Ban-galore. In addition to testing the knowledge and awareness of students, the events are designed to help them improve their communication and marketing skills as well. This inter - collegiate techno - managerial extravaganza from UVCE has been received very well and has attracted a lot of crowd over the past years.
